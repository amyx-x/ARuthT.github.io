Digital Art

After acheiving my level 5 diploma in creative computing, my artwork took a turn towards technology. Using these newly found skills, I have explored projects with visual programming software TouchDesigner, 3D sculpting software Blender, and HTML/ CSS/ JS coding in Visual Studio Code. In addition to this, I investigated the use of old technology such as old JVC broadcast monitors and other ways of digital/ technological display. This body of work explores the connection between human and machine ...

Graduate Show, Interactive Installation

2026 | Touch Designer, Blender, LED panel, Printed Photography

This art installation is a multimedia collective demonstrating related themes around surrealism and the unconscious mind. This is shown via dreams; representation and symbolism of specific objects, some of which will be displayed on LED light panels. Further aspects include data from the brain; a process meant to explore the conscious and unconscious interactivity between human and machine. -this can be seen via the muse 2 headset setup, of which viewers are encouraged to wear. As they dawn the headset, the video art on the JVC television will distort and become unfamiliar with the use of their own brain waves. The conscious interaction includes the option to press keyboard buttons which will change the presentation of the screen or the way that their brainwaves alter the video.

Eye Tracking, User interaction

2025 | Touchdesigner, Blender, Digital Photography |

Visual Programming piece with photographic elements. The artwork uses a python script to gather eye movement data using a webcamera. This data then gets input into the software where it can change/ distort the artwork. ultimatly creating a space for interaction where the viewer not only views the technological process but rather becomes entangled within it.
